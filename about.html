About sahaba

Discover our commitment to pure, quality water

Our Story

sahaba was founded with a simple mission: to provide the purest, most refreshing water to families across the UAE. Our name, meaning "cloud" in Arabic, represents the natural cycle of water - from clouds to earth, purified and ready to nourish.

We believe that access to clean, pure water is a fundamental right. That's why we've invested in state-of-the-art purification technology and sustainable practices to bring you water that's not just safe, but exceptional.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us every day

Purity First

Every drop of sahaba water goes through rigorous multi-stage filtration to ensure absolute purity and safety for your family.

Environmental Care

Community Focus

Supporting local communities and ensuring everyone has access to clean, affordable drinking water is at the heart of what we do.
